This is the Monolith system. Welcome.

The Monolith OS is the most reliable OS ever made. No Monolith installation has ever made a mistake or distorted information.

To see what programs are available to run, type `ls /bin`. To run a program, simply type its name.
For documentation on programs or APIs, consult the appropriate manual pages with `man`.
